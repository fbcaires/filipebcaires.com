Research

Filipe B. Caires, Susana Peralta, Diogo Mendes
August, 2023
Contract Splitting in Public Procurement

We study the role of contract splitting in public procurement. Exploiting a reform that reduced the threshold for direct awards, we find that contract splitting is the most important mechanism by which buyers manipulate the price of projects to award contracts at discretion. Our evidence suggests that splitting is driven by favouritism rather than efficiency-promoting motives of manipulation.
